\m \bd Faarfannaa \bd*\bd 29\bd*
\s2 \bdit \zbr\bdit*Aangoon Waaqayyoo Qaqawwee Keessaan Mul'achuu Isaa (Faarfannaa Daawit)
\m \bd 1\bd* Yaa warra waaqa irraa, Waaqayyoof ulfina dhi'eessaa! Aangoo Waaqayyoos beeksisaa.
\m \bd 2\bd* Ulfina maqaa isaatiif ta'u Waaqayyoof dhi'eessaa! Qulqullummaa akka mi'a miidhaginaatti naqadhaatii Waaqayyoof sagadaa! 
\m \bd 3\bd* Sagaleen Waaqayyoo galaana irraa in dhaga'ama, Waaqayyo inni ulfina-qabeessi in qaqawweessa'a; inni galaana gurguddaa irrattis waaqayyummaa isaa in argisiisa.
\m \bd 4\bd* Sagaleen Waaqayyoo humnaan in dhaga'ama; sagaleen Waaqayyoo surraadhaan in sochoosa.
\m \bd 5\bd* Sagaleen Waaqayyoo muka gaattiraa in caccabsa; Waaqayyo muka gaattiraa tulloota Libaanon in caccabsa.
\m \bd 6\bd* Inni tulloota Libaanon akka jabbii, tulluu Siiriyonis akka dibicha gafarsaa in burraaqsisa.
\m \bd 7\bd* Sagaleen Waaqayyoo balaqqeessa akka ibiddaa in finiinsa; 
\m \bd 8\bd* sagaleen Waaqayyoo lafa duwwaa in sochoosa, Waaqayyo lafa duwwaa isa kan Qaadesh in sochoosa.
\m \bd 9\bd* Sagaleen Waaqayyoo qilxuu of irra in naannessa, mukkeetii caakkaa irraas baalaa fi damee in cira; mana qulqullummaa isaa keessatti immoo hundinuu hooqubaa in dhi'eessu.
\m \bd 10\bd* Waaqayyo bishaan badiisaa gararraa iyyuu taa'eera; Waaqayyo bara baraan mootii ta'ee teessoo irra in taa'a.
\m \bd 11\bd* Waaqayyo saba isaatiif jabina haa kennu; Waaqayyo saba isaa nagaadhaan haa eebbisu!
