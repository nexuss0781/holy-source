\m \bd Faarfannaa \bd*\bd 31\bd*
\s2 \bdit \zbr\bdit*Gaarummaan Waaqayyoo Warra Kooluu Isatti Galaniif Baay'ee Dha (Geggeessituu warra faarfatootaaf; faarfannaa Daawit)
\m \bd 1\bd* Yaa Waaqayyo, ani kooluu sitti galeera, ati yoom iyyuu akka ani hin yeellaasifamne godhi, ati qajeelaa dha, ana oolchi.
\m \bd 2\bd* Gurra kee gara kootti qabi, ariifadhuu kana jalaa na baasi, ati kattaa ani itti baqadhee oolu anaaf ta'i, ana oolchuufis mana masaraa jabaa anaaf ta'i!
\m \bd 3\bd* Ati kattaa, masaraas anaaf taateetta; maqaa keetiif jedhii karaa irra ana buusi, ana geggeessis.
\m \bd 4\bd* Ati hoofkalchaa koo waan taateef, kiyyoo anaaf dhoksan keessaa na baasi.
\m \bd 5\bd* Yaa gooftaa, ati Waaqayyo amanamaa dha, ati ana furteetta; ani immoo hafuura koo harka keetti nan kennadha.
\m \bd 6\bd* Namoota waaqayyolii tolfaman warra waa'ee hin baafne safeeffatan, ani nan jibba, ofii kootii garuu Waaqayyoon nan amanadha.
\m \bd 7\bd* Ati gadadamuu koo argiteetta, muddama lubbuu koos anaaf beekteetta; kanaaf sagalee gammachuu nan dhageessisa, gaarummaa keettis nan gammada.
\m \bd 8\bd* Miilli koo iddoo bal'aa irra qajeelee akka dhaabatu in goote malee, ati harka diinota kootti dabarsitee ana hin kennine.
\m \bd 9\bd* Yaa Waaqayyo, ati araara anatti argisiisi! Ani rakkinattan jira, gadda irraa kan ka'e iji koo arguu dadhabe, lubbuun koo, namummaan koos gad in adeeme.
\m \bd 10\bd* Barri jireenya koo gaddaan, waggoonni koos aadnaadhaan darbaniiru; humni koo yakka koo irraa kan ka'e laafeera; lafeen koos qorqameera.
\m \bd 11\bd* warra na cunqursan duratti arrabsoodhaaf, olloota kootiifis muka ga'oo nan ta'e; warra ana beekan dhagnatu suuka'a; warri karaatti ana argan na baqatu. 
\m \bd 12\bd* Ani akka nama du'eetti yaada namaa keessaa irraanfatameera, akka qodaa cabaas ta'eera.
\m \bd 13\bd* ''Doorsisi karaa hundumaa isa naanna'ee jira'' kan jedhu, hasaasa diinota baay'ee nan dhaga'a; wal ga'anii ana irratti mari'atu, lubbuu koo baasuufis anatti in malu. 
\m \bd 14\bd* Ani si amanadheera yaa Waaqayyo, ''Ati Waaqayyo koo ti'' jedheera.
\m \bd 15\bd* Barri koo hundinuu si harka jira, harka warra diina anatti ta'anii fi warra na ari'atanii na oolchi.
\m \bd 16\bd* Fuulli kee garbicha kee irratti akka ibsu godhi, gaarummaa keetiin ana gargaari.
\m \bd 17\bd* Yaa Waaqayyo, ani si waammadheera; ani akka hin yeellaasifamne anaaf godhi! Warri jal'oonni haa yeellaasifaman, gara iddoo lafa jalaatti gad bu'anii, calluma haa jechisiifaman.
\m \bd 18\bd* Afaan sobduun kooraa fi tuffiidhaan salphisee nama qajeelaa irratti dubbatu haa hidhamu.
\m \bd 19\bd* Wanti gaariin inni ati warra si sodaataniif keesse, gaarummaan inni ati ilmaan namootaa duratti warra kooluu sitti galanitti argisiifte, attam baay'ee dha.
\m \bd 20\bd* Ati waan namoonni koratanittii fuula kee duratti golbooba keetiin isaan in eegda; qoccolloo duraas godoo kee keessa isaan in dhoksita.
\m \bd 21\bd* Yeroo ani mandara marfame keessa turetti, Waaqayyo gaarummaa isaa isa dinqisiisaa na argisiiseera; kanaaf maqaan isaa haa eebbifamu.
\m \bd 22\bd* Ani illee yeroon waanan godhu wallaale, ''Ani si arguu irraa citeera'' jedheen ture; ati garuu sagalee himata koo, isa ani gargaarsaaf sitti iyyadhe anaaf dhageesseetta.
\m \bd 23\bd* Isin warri isaaf of kennitan hundinuu Waaqayyoon jaalladhaa! Waaqayyo warra amanamoo in eega, koortuudhaaf garuu isa inni hojjete irra dabarsee in deebisaaf.
\m \bd 24\bd* Isin warri Waaqayyoon eeggattan hundinuu, jabaadhaa, laphee jabaadhaas!
