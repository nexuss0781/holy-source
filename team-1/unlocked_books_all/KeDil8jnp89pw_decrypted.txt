\m \bd Faarfannaa \bd*\bd 37\bd*
\s2 \bdit \zbr\bdit*Warra Jal'ootaaf Jireenyi Toluun Warra Qajeelota Hin Wallaalchisin (Kan Daawit)
\m \bd 1\bd* Warra waan jal'aa hojjetanitti hin aarin, waan isaan hamaa godhanii argatanittis hin hinaafin.
\m \bd 2\bd* Isaan akka margaatti dafanii in coolligu; akka biqiltuu misee goguuttis in golgolaa'u.
\m \bd 3\bd* Waaqayyoon amanadhu, wanta gaarii ta'es hojjedhu; biyyuma keessa nagaadhaan taa'i, amanamummaas eeggadhu.
\m \bd 4\bd* Waaqayyotti gammachuu qabaadhu! Inni immoo kadhata garaa kee siif in kenna.
\m \bd 5\bd* Karaa kee Waaqayyotti kennadhu, isa amanadhu! Innis siif in raawwata.
\m \bd 6\bd* Inni qajeelummaa kee akka ifaatti, dhugaa kees akka aduu saafaatti, bakkeetti in baasa.
\m \bd 7\bd* Waaqayyo duratti gab jedhi, obsaanis isa eeggadhu, nama karaa isaa wal qixxeeffatetti, nama wanta hamaa koratee fiixaan baafatettis hin aarin! 
\m \bd 8\bd* Dheekkamuu irraa of dhowwi, banbanuus dhiisi, inni hamaa hojjechuu duwwaatti waan si geessuuf hin aarin.
\m \bd 9\bd* Warri jal'oonni lafa irraa in balleeffamu, warri Waaqayyoon eeggatan garuu lafa in qabaatu. 
\m \bd 10\bd* Inni jal'aan yeroo xinnoo booddee achitti hin argamu, iddoo inni ture hubattee yoo ilaalte, inni hin jiru.
\m \bd 11\bd* Warri garraamonni garuu lafa in qabaatu, isaanis nagaa guututti jiraachuu isaaniitiin sonaan in gammadu.
\m \bd 12\bd* Namni jal'aan nama qajeelaatti in mala, ittis in ciniinnata.
\m \bd 13\bd* guyyaan namicha jal'aa akka dhi'aate waan arguuf, gooftichi namichatti in kolfa.
\m \bd 14\bd* Warri jal'oonni, nama gadadamaa fi dhabaa lafaan dha'uudhaaf, warra karaa qajeelaa irra jiranis ajjeesuudhaaf billaa isaanii in luqqifatu, iddaa isaaniis in dabsatu.
\m \bd 15\bd* Billaan isaanii garaadhuma isaaniitti gala, iddaan isaaniis in caccaba.
\m \bd 16\bd* Qabeenya guddaa jal'oonni baay'een qaban irra, qabeenya xinnaa namni qajeelaan qabu wayya.
\m \bd 17\bd* Irreen jal'ootaa in caccaba, warra qajeelota garuu Waaqayyo qabaa isaa keessatti in eega.
\m \bd 18\bd* Waaqayyo bara jireenya warra balleessaa hin qabnee beekee, yaada jaalalaatiin duukaa in bu'a, wanti isaan itti darbanii argachuuf jiranis kan isaanii ta'ee in hafa;
\m \bd 19\bd* bara gadheetti hin yeella'an, bara beelaattis in quufu.
\m \bd 20\bd* Warri jal'oonni garuu badduu in ba'u; diinonni Waaqayyoo akka miidhagina margaa ti, akkuma aarri badu, isaanis in badu.
\m \bd 21\bd* Namni jal'aan in liqeeffata, danda'ee hin baasus; namni qajeelaan immoo arjaa dha, waan kennus qaba.
\m \bd 22\bd* Warri Waaqayyo eebbise lafa in qabaatu; warri inni abaare immoo lafa irraa in balleeffamu.
\m \bd 23\bd* Ejjennaan namaa inni Waaqayyo jabeessu isa biraa in ka'a; karaan isaa sunis gooftaatti in tola, \bd 24\bd* Waaqayyo harka isaa qabee waan isa eeguuf, inni yoo kufe iyyuu, lafa hin ga'u.
\m \bd 25\bd* Dargaggummaa argeera, dulluma biras ga'eera; garuu namni qajeelaan utuu gatamuu, ijoolleen isaas waan nyaatan dhabanii utuu kadhatanii ani takkaa argee hin beeku.
\m \bd 26\bd* Inni yoom iyyuu in arjooma, in liqeessas, ijoolleen isaas eebbaaf in ta'u.
\m \bd 27\bd* Wanta hamaa irraa fagaadhu, wanta gaarii hojjedhu! Yoos bara baraan nagaatti in teessa.
\m \bd 28\bd* Waaqayyo waan dhugaa ta'e in jaallata, warra isaaf of kennanis hin gatu; isaan bara baraan in eegamu, ijoolleen jal'ootaa immoo lafa irraa in balleeffamu.
\m \bd 29\bd* Warri qajeelonni biyya in qabaatu, yeroo hundumaafis keessa in taa'u.
\m \bd 30\bd* Afaan nama qajeelaa ogummaadhaan in dubbata, arrabni isaas waan dhugaa ta'e in hima.
\m \bd 31\bd* seerri Waaqayyo isaatii garaa isaa keessa jira, faanni isaas isa irraa hin mittiqu.
\m \bd 32\bd* Namni jal'aan riphee nama qajeelaa in eeggata, isa ajjeesuus in barbaada.
\m \bd 33\bd* Waaqayyo garuu harka isaatti isa hin kennu, yommuu namni itti faraduuf jedhus yakka itti hin mursiisu. 
\m \bd 34\bd* Waaqayyoon abdiidhaan eeggadhu, karaa isaa irraa akka hin jal'annes eeggadhu! Biyya akka qabaattuuf ulfina siif in kenna, lafa irraa balleeffamuu warra jal'ootaas argitee in gammadda.
\m \bd 35\bd* Nama jal'aa humnaan nama irratti ka'u ani argeera, inni akka muka lafti itti tolee dagaagee ta'e; 
\m \bd 36\bd* ergasii yeroon achiin darbutti, ilaalee nan dhabe; ani isa nan barbaade, inni immoo hin argamne.
\m \bd 37\bd* Nama hir'ina hin qabne caqasi, nama yaada qajeelaas ilaali! Qacceen nama nagaa hin badu.
\m \bd 38\bd* Warri irra-daddarbituun garuu wal duukaa in duguugamu, qacceen warra jal'ootaas lafa irraa in balleeffamu.
\m \bd 39\bd* Fureen qajeelotaa Waaqayyo biraa in dhufa, Waaqayyo yeroo rakkinaatti da'oo isaanii ti.
\m \bd 40\bd* Waaqayyo isaan in gargaara, isaan in oolchas; waan isaan kooluu isatti galaniif, inni harka warra jal'ootaa duraa oolcheeisaan in fura.
