\m \bd Faarfannaa \bd*\bd 81\bd*
\s2 \bdit \zbr\bdit*Waaqayyo Nama Isaaf Dhaga'u Barbaaduu Isaa (Geggeessituu warra faarfatootaaf; kun kiraara guddaadhaan yookiis akka qooqa faarfannaa kanaan dura beekame tokkootti faarfatame; kan Asaaf)
\m \bd 1\bd* Waaqayyo aangoo keenya waan ta'eef, ililchaa! Waaqayyo isa kan Yaaqoobitti gammaduu keessan dhageessisaa!
\m \bd 2\bd* Faarfannaa jalqabaa, dubbisa rukutaa, maseenqoo namatti toluu fi kiraara dhageessisaa.
\m \bd 3\bd* Baatii ji'aatti, goobana ji'aatti, guyyaa ayyaana keenyaattis fiinoo afuufaa.
\m \bd 4\bd* Inni kun Israa'eliif seerrata, Waaqayyo isa kan Yaaqoob birattis, wanta qajeelaa ta'ee fudhatamee dha.
\m \bd 5\bd* Yeroo biyya Gibxii rukutuuf ba'e, inni abboommii isaaf dhugaa ba'u ijoollee Yoseef gidduu dhaabe; anis sagalee dur hin beekne tokko nan dhaga'e.
\m \bd 6\bd* Innis, "Gatiittii isaa ba'aa jalaa nan baase, harka isaas guuboo baachuu nan dhiisise.
\m \bd 7\bd* Ati yeroo rakkinaatti in waammatte, ani immoo keessaa sin baase, golgaa duumessa qaqawwee qabu keessaa siif dhaga'eera; bishaan morkii sana biratti si qoreera.
\m \bd 8\bd* Dhaggeeffadhu yaa saba ko, ani waanan si gorsu qaba; yaa Israa'el, utuu anaaf dhageessee, attam gaarii dha.
\m \bd 9\bd* Waaqayyolii warra ormaa keessaa tokko illee si keessatti hin argamin, waaqayyolii warra kaaniifis hin sagadin! 
\m \bd 10\bd* Ani Waaqayyo gooftaa kee ti, anatu biyya Gibxiitii si baase; afaan kee as qabadhu, ani immoo siif nan guuta.
\m \bd 11\bd* "Sabni koo garuu sagalee koo dhaggeeffachuu in dide, Israa'el yaada kootti galuu hin barbaadne.
\m \bd 12\bd* Isaan mata-jabeeyyii akka ta'aniif nan dhiise; yaada isaanii isa qopheessan duukaas in bu'an. 
\m \bd 13\bd* Sabni koo utuu anaaf dhaga'ee, Israa'el utuu karaa koo irra adeemee, \bd 14\bd* silaa ani immoo diinota isaa salphaatti nan gugguufsisa, hamajaajii isaa irrattis harka koo nan garagalfadha. 
\m \bd 15\bd* Warri Waaqayyoon jibban isaaf in gugguufu, yeroon isaaniis hamma bara baraatti kanumaan dhumuu isaa ti.
\m \bd 16\bd* Ani immoo qamadii isa caaluun sin soora, damma holqa kattaa keessaa ba'uunis sin quufsa" jedhe.
