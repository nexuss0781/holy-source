\m \bd Faarfannaa \bd*\bd 73\bd*
\s2 \bdit \zbr\bdit*Waaqayyoon Qabaachuun Waan Hundumaa Irra Caaluu Isaa Bira Ga'uu(Faarfannaa Asaaf)
\m \bd 1\bd* Waaqayyo saba Israa'eliif, warra garaan isaanii qullaa'aa ta'eef dhuguma gaarii dha.
\m \bd 2\bd* Waa'ee kootiif yoo ta'e garuu, miilli koo bitintiree kufuu ga'eera, ejjennaan koos mucucaachuuf xinnootu hafe.
\m \bd 3\bd* Warri jal'oonni nagaatti jiraachuu isaanii yeroon arge, warra of jajan kanatti weenna'een ture.
\m \bd 4\bd* Isaan dhiphina hin qaban, dhagni isaanii fayyaa dha, gabbataa dhas;
\m \bd 5\bd* rakkinni nama irra ga'u isaan irra hin geenye, akka namoota biraas dha'ichi isaanitti hin buune.
\m \bd 6\bd* Kanaaf of koorsuu akka naqata mormaatti in kaa'atu, humnaan nama irratti ka'uus akka wayyaatti in uffatu;
\m \bd 7\bd* ija isaanii coomatu bobbokokse, inni isaan garaatti yaadan guutee irra dhangala'e.
\m \bd 8\bd* isaan in ga'isu humnaan waa'ee nama cunqursuu in dubbatu, nama gararraa goraniis achii gad in dubbatu.
\m \bd 9\bd* Afaaniin bantii waaqaa bira in ga'u, arrabni isaaniis biyya lafaa waliin in ga'a.
\m \bd 10\bd* kanaaf sabni Waaqayyoo illee isaanitti in gora, waanuma isaan jedhanis safara malee akka bishaanii in dhuga.
\m \bd 11\bd* Isaan immoo, "Waaqayyo attamitti beekuu danda'a ree? Isa hundumaa gararraa biras waa beekuun jiraa ree?" in jedhu.
\m \bd 12\bd* Ilaa! Jal'oonni warra akkasii ti, yeroo hundumaa of irraa bu'anii in jiraatu, jabaachaas in adeemu.
\m \bd 13\bd* Yoos immoo ani ofumaan garaa koo qulqullinatti eeggachuuf dhama'ee ree? Yakka akkan hin qabne argisiisuufis ofumaan harka koo dhiqadhee ree?
\m \bd 14\bd* Guyyaa guyyaatti dha'ichi anatti bu'eera, ganama ganamas adabamuun na eeggata ture.
\m \bd 15\bd* Utuun, "Akkuma isaanii nan dubbadha" jedheera ta'ee, dhaloota ijoolleen kee keessa jiran biratti amanamuu dhabee, isaanis akka garagaggalan nan godhan ture.
\m \bd 16\bd* Yeroo ani kana hubachuuf jedhee itti yaade immoo, rakkisaa ta'ee anatti argame.
\m \bd 17\bd* Hamma ani mana qulqullummaa Waaqayyootti galee, jarri sun gara dhumaatti maal akka ta'an hubachuu bira ga'etti, na rakkise.
\m \bd 18\bd* Dhuguma iyyuu ati lafa mucucaatu irra isaan in dhaabachiifta, kukkufanii akka caccabanis in goota.
\m \bd 19\bd* Attamitti battalatti badiisi sodaachisaan isaan irra ga'e, attamittis dhidhimanii naasuu guddaadhaan xaph jedhan!
\m \bd 20\bd* Akka wanti abjuudhaan argan yeroo dammaqan dhabamu, akkasumas yaa gooftaa, ati yommuu kaatu, bifa isaanii akka waan hin jirreetti in ilaalta.
\m \bd 21\bd* Yommuu jireenyi anatti hadhaa'e, yeroo onnee na tumates,
\m \bd 22\bd* ani nama gowwaa, nama waa hubachuu hin dandeenye ta'ee, si duratti akkuma horii marga dheeduun ture.
\m \bd 23\bd* Haa ta'u iyyuu malee, ani yeroo hundumaa si biratti nan hafa, atis harka koo mirgaa in qabda;
\m \bd 24\bd* ati gorsa keetiin ana in geggeessita, kana booddees ulfinatti ana in fudhatta.
\m \bd 25\bd* Waaqa irratti si malee eenyuun nan qabaadha ree? Lafa irrattis si malee maalan eeggadha ree?
\m \bd 26\bd* Foon koo, keessi namummaa koos yoo gad dhuman iyyuu, Waaqayyo garaa koo in jajjabeessa, bara baraanis qooda anaaf in ta'a.
\m \bd 27\bd* Kunoo, warri si irraa fagaatan in balleeffamu, warra amanamuu dhiisanii kan biraatti goranis ati in fixxa.
\m \bd 28\bd* Waa'ee koof yoo ta'e garuu, Waaqayyotti dhi'aachuun anaaf gaarii dha; yaa Waaqayyo gooftaa, hojii kee hundumaa himuudhaaf, ani da'oo si godhadheera.
